About Us

CSIT 2K76 Community

CSIT 2k76 is a non-profitable, non-governmental, non-political, social organization established in 2019 by young energetic students persudaing their career in Computer Science of 2076 batch. The main purpose of establishing 2k76 CSIT is to become more productive and be ready for tech industries in Nepal and globally. We will actively involve for the upcoming days to make it more productive.
